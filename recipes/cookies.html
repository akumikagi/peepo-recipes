Chocolate Cookies

Expected look (source: buzzfeed.com)

Juicy chocolate-filled cookies!

Sweet baked snack with butter flavor and chocolate chips all over.

Ingredients:

  • Granulated sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Milk or semi-sweet chocolate
  • Dark chocolate

Steps:

  1. Whisk together the sugars and butter
  2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla
  3. Sift in the flour and baking soda
  4. Fold in the chocolate chunks
  5. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C)
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes
