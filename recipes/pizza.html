Ingredients:

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices of peppeoroni
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Steps:

  1. Preaheat the oven
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve
